Welcome to the team

The Suisse Cheese application is a HackZurich application that tackles the latest demands of the bank, ensuring that clients are getting their needs met in new and innovative ways.

What challenges do we tackle?

Clients are increasingly becoming disconnected from their portfolio's and impactful real-world events dueo to information overflow in banking. We ensure that only the most relevant topics are seen by users, to ensure that they know what their next steps are.
